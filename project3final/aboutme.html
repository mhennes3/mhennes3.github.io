Matthew W. Hennessey

University of Notre Dame

As a kid, I was very artistic, and I passionately pursued many creative endeavors. When I reached Notre Dame, I missed having a creative outlet. I heard about the Collaborative Innovation and Design Minor and thought it the perfect opportunity to learn new skills while expressing my creative side. Through the minor, I have had the opportunity to take many design courses, including Visual Communication Design (VCD) 1, VCD3: Web Design, and Collaborative Design Development.
